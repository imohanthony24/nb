Are Newbury Land Financial of Newcastle (Newbury Land) accounts FCA insured?

Yes. Newbury Land Newcastle is a member of the FCA, so our deposit accounts are insured up to the maximum amount allowed by law. If you want to learn more about FCA insurance limits, visit www.fca.org.uk

Are there fees for Newbury Land accounts?

There are no monthly maintenance or annual fees charged for Newbury Land accounts. Certain circumstances, like early withdrawal of CDs or insufficient funds, may incur penalties or fees. For more information about fees, please see account Terms and Conditions.

What is an “external” account?

Your external account is a bank account you have with another U.K. financial institution. It’s linked to your Newbury Land account so that you can transfer money between the two. Any external account that is linked to your Newbury Land account must be in an account holder’s name. This includes payment processing companies such as: PayPal, Dwolla, Coinbase, Venmo, Square, and Payday loan deposits.

Tell me about “test deposits”.

For security reasons, we use test deposits as part of the external account verification process. 2 to 5 business days after you add a link to your Newbury Land account, we’ll make two test deposits of less than $1.00 into your external account. We do not use your money to make these test deposits. We’ll then ask you to log in to your Newbury Land account and report the amounts of those test deposits. After you have confirmed the test deposit amounts, you’ll be able to begin transferring money between your Newbury Land account and your external account. We use this method to verify each new external account. If you do not verify your external account within 99 days from when you opened your account, transfers will be restricted until you can verify your external bank account.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your Newbury Land Online Banking password on our website. Simply select the Forgot UserID and/or Password link during Log In from the Newbury Land Online Banking home page. You will be asked a few short questions to verify your identity. Then choose to have a one time pass code sent to you by email, phone, or text. The one time pass code will be active for 10 minutes.

What is Newbury Land Online Bank swift code/BIC number?

Our swift code/BIC number is NFANUB5S Please note: Barclays does not accept wire transfers at this time.

How do I setup a direct deposit?

To set up direct deposit, simply contact the external account from which you’d like to transfer funds into your Newbury Land account. You’ll need to follow their procedures for setting up direct deposit but all of them will need the following information: · Your Newbury Land Online Banking account number · Our swift code/BIC number: NFANUB5S

How can I update my personal address or other information?

There are two ways to update your personal information: · Log in to our Newbury Land website and sign in to your account to change your personal information, or · Give our Customer Care team a call at 179-272-0606. We’re here to help you, 7 days a week from 8:00 am to 8:00 pm GMT.
